Camera

This is a simple camera created using HTML, CSS and Javascript. In it you first need to give camera permission to the website (dont be afraid to give permission, its totally safe website). Then you have to click on the "Take a picture" button and your photo will come in the right corner. Then you can save the picture by a right click and click on "save image as" button on the pop-up or in mobile just have a long click and a pop-up will appear and save the image (in android)...
You can Test it in your browser by clicking Test button or download .zip file, extract it and then run it...
